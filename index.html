About

I became a software engineer because I've always loved working with new technologies. I thrive in dynamic and collaborative environments and find enjoyment in creating efficient solutions to complex problems.

I'm proficient in several programming languages, including Python, C++, JavaScript, and SQL. I have extensive experience in full-stack web development, mobile development and cloud computing. Recently, I've started delving into the realm of machine learning, looking to expand my skills and apply these techniques to real-world challenges. View my resume for more information.

Outside of work a lot of my time is spent doing jiu jitsu—a sport and an art. I'm happiest when taking quiet walks at night or eating good food.
